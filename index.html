About Us

The PerCina Report was created by lenders and investors with
the single objective of helping your company position itself
more effectively when you are looking to secure new capital.

The PerCina Report name is derived from the Latin phrase “Periculum SarCina” – “Risk Package”.
The “risk package” or credit or investment memorandum, is the summary analysis that lenders
and investors create from the information you have provided them to decide whether or not to
provide you with capital. This is YOUR STORY.

Let our technology and know-how help YOU tell it more effectively!

Why should this matter?

Most of the information required to underwrite your company for investment resides with your company, not with 3rd party sources / credit bureaus.

Credit approving loans requires possessing all the relevant information - today this is largely an inefficient, manual, time consuming process controlled by lenders.

Underwriting is company specific. Why let the lender create the wrong story of your company?

Your company’s story needs to be effectively communicated - requiring the right data upfront and a fair assessment of risks and upside.

Companies successfully secure the capital they need when they are in command of their data, business issues and story line, and have effectively communicated this to lenders and investors.

How the Percina Report platform helps companies.

Our technology puts the power of borrowing back in the hands of the borrower by DIGITIZING the way borrowers:

  • • CREATE their own company story
  • • COMMUNICATE their story, more effectively
  • • In a way lenders need to see it.

PerCina enables companies to create a web based, secure & confidential, best in class, investment memorandum, inclusive of financial analysis including charts, ratios and risks.

The tool provides additional guidance, illustrations and the most effective way to present your company to lenders.

Lenders and intermediaries also can utilize the tool for increased efficiencies and syndications – complete with seamless edit rights.
